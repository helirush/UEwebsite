## Q&A Quick Answers
### What problem does Unity solve?
Unity addresses hidden electrical field burden at industrial 480V sites, especially reactive burden, harmonic distortion, and thermal stress that degrade performance and efficiency.

### What is MPTS in Unity language?
MPTS is Unity’s harmonization method at the load level that improves field behavior and supports better conversion quality.

### Why “Measure, Manage, Exchange”?
It defines Unity’s workflow from visibility to control to coordinated system response.

### Is Unity just software?
No. Unity combines field engineering, harmonization technology, and intelligence-driven reporting in operating environments.

### What is the tone expectation for public explanation?
Industrial, concrete, and operationally grounded. Avoid abstract-only language and avoid internal production notes in public-facing copy.
