ARMI Fayetteville Facility Overview

The ARMI Fayetteville site is configured as a single monitored 500 kVA transformer field. This portal is structured to receive monthly study outputs produced by eBehavior and published to eWebmaster, following the same customer journey style used in Cherry Avenue.

Each monthly release will present a two-part Summaryboard perspective: (1) meter-side transformer behavior and (2) H240 MPTS harmonization behavior, enabling direct comparison of electrical field performance and harmonization impact.
